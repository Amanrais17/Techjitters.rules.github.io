Event : IT Quiz Inter-college Event In Offline Mode

IT Quiz Rules


• Team size – 2 (both members from same institution)
• In case of any dispute, Panel’s or Judge’s decision will be final
Three rounds:
Round one - screening round. 25 Questions (MCQs only) with a time limit of 30 minutes. All the participating teams eligible
Round two - intermediate round. 10 Questions (MCQs only) with a time limit of 10 minutes. Top 12 teams eligible
Round three - Final round. 7 Questions with a time limit of 20 minutes. Top 6 teams eligible. The final round to be divided into ….2…. subsections (MCQs + Problem Solving).
Top 3 teams will be awarded.
